## DORA Compliance

Twingate's support for the EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) for regulated financial entities.

**Key Information:**
- Twingate offers a DORA Addendum for Business and Enterprise subscribers
- The addendum contains contractual provisions required by DORA-regulated entities for ICT vendor agreements
- Contact the Twingate customer team to obtain a copy

**Prerequisites:**
- Business or Enterprise subscription required
