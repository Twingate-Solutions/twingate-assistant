# DORA Compliance

## Summary
Twingate supports financial entities regulated by the EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA). A DORA Addendum is available for Business and Enterprise subscribers, providing required contractual provisions for ICT vendor agreements.

## Key Information
- DORA Addendum covers contractual provisions required between DORA-regulated entities and ICT vendors
- Available to **Business** and **Enterprise** subscription tiers only
- Must contact Twingate's customer team to obtain the addendum

## Prerequisites
- Active Business or Enterprise Twingate subscription
- Organization must be subject to DORA regulation (financial entities in scope)

## Configuration Values
None — this is a contractual/compliance document, not a technical configuration.

## Gotchas
- Free/Starter tier customers are **not** eligible for the DORA Addendum
- Addendum is not self-serve; requires direct contact with Twingate's customer team
