# DORA Compliance

## Page Title
DORA Compliance

## Summary
Twingate supports financial entities regulated by the Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) through a contractual DORA Addendum. This addendum satisfies ICT vendor agreement requirements mandated by DORA for regulated customers.

## Key Information
- DORA Addendum is available for **Business** and **Enterprise** subscription tiers only
- Addendum contains contractual provisions required by DORA for ICT vendor agreements
- Must contact Twingate's customer team to obtain the addendum
- Twingate is classified as an ICT vendor under DORA's scope

## Prerequisites
- Active Twingate **Business** or **Enterprise** subscription
- Organization must be subject to DORA regulation (EU financial entities)

## Configuration Values
None — this is a contractual/compliance process, not a technical configuration.

## Step-by-Step
1. Verify your subscription is Business or Enterprise tier
2. Contact Twingate customer team to request the DORA Addendum
3. Execute the addendum as part of your ICT vendor agreement with Twingate

## Gotchas
- Free and Starter tier customers cannot obtain the DORA Addendum — subscription upgrade required
- No self-service option; addendum requires direct engagement with Twingate's customer team
