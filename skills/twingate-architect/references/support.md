# Twingate Support Options

## Summary
Twingate provides tiered support based on subscription plan. Business and Enterprise plans receive formal technical support; lower tiers rely on self-service resources and community forums.

## Key Information
- **Help Center**: Primary self-service knowledge base
- **Community**: Twingate Subreddit for peer support
- **Billing**: Handled via Subscription Management page
- **Technical Support**: Break-fix only, Business and Enterprise plans exclusively
- **No direct support** for Starter, Teams, or trial Business accounts

## Support Channels by Plan

| Plan | Support Available |
|------|------------------|
| Enterprise | Technical Support (break-fix) |
| Business (paid) | Technical Support (break-fix) |
| Business (trial) | Community/docs only |
| Teams | Community/docs only |
| Starter | Community/docs only |

## Contact Points
- **Technical Support requests**: Via [Technical Support Services](https://www.twingate.com/docs/support) page (Business/Enterprise)
- **Pre-sales (with sales rep)**: Route through your sales representative
- **Pre-sales (self-serve trial)**: `onboarding@twingate.com`
- **Community support**: Twingate Subreddit

## Gotchas
- Trial Business accounts do **not** qualify for technical support despite being the "Business" tier
- Technical support is **break-fix only** — not general product guidance or troubleshooting for unsupported tiers
- Self-serve trial customers without a sales rep must use email or community; no direct support queue access
