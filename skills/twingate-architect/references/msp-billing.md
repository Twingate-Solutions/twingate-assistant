## MSP Billing

Consolidated monthly billing for Managed Service Providers. All Customer Networks are billed together on the 1st of each calendar month.

**License Calculation:**
- Licenses = total Users + Service Accounts across all Customer Networks **minus** admins
- Each Customer Network has a minimum of one license regardless of headcount
- Example: 100 users + service accounts, 5 admins = 95 licenses billed

**Billing Management (MSP Portal → Settings → Billing → Manage Plan):**
- Child subscriptions: view per-Customer-Network line items and monthly cost
- Payment Methods: update credit card
- Billing History: view/download invoices

**Key Information:**
- Invoices sent only to the MSP email (not to end customers)
- Billing pages are only visible in the MSP portal — not in Customer Network Admin Consoles
- To change a Customer Network plan: contact partnersupport@twingate.com
- To update invoice email: MSP portal Billing → Manage → Account Information
