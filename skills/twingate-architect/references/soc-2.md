## SOC 2 Report

Twingate holds a SOC 2 Type 2 report — an independent third-party audit of internal controls covering security and Trust Services Criteria, tested over a period of time (not a point-in-time snapshot).

**Key Information:**
- Type 2 designation means controls were tested over a period of time
- Report available at trust.twingate.com (existing customers) — access requests reviewed by Twingate team; access expires after 30 days
- For further information, contact the Twingate customer team
