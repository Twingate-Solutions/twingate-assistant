# Twingate Trust Center

## Summary
The Twingate Trust Center is a centralized hub for security, privacy, legal, and compliance documentation. It provides transparency into Twingate's security practices, certifications, and legal agreements for customers evaluating or auditing the platform.

## Key Information

- **Security documentation**: Security information and responsible disclosure policy available
- **Service reliability**: Real-time service status page accessible
- **Compliance certifications**: SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS, GDPR, DORA
- **Privacy notices**: General Privacy Policy and California Supplemental Privacy Notice (CCPA)
- **Legal agreements**: Customer Agreement and User Terms of Service
- **Additional resources**: W-9 form and Twingate & Customer Data document available

## Available Documents

| Category | Documents |
|----------|-----------|
| Security | Security Information, Responsible Disclosure Policy |
| Compliance | SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS, GDPR, DORA |
| Privacy | Privacy Policy, California Supplemental Privacy Notice |
| Legal | Customer Agreement, User Terms of Service |
| Other | W-9 Form, Twingate & Customer Data |

## Prerequisites
- None — Trust Center is publicly accessible
- Contact Twingate directly for compliance certifications/standards not listed

## Gotchas
- Compliance certifications not listed on the page require direct contact with Twingate
- SOC 2 Report may require a request/NDA to access the full report (not explicitly stated on this page)
- Page content updates periodically ("Last updated 8 days ago")
