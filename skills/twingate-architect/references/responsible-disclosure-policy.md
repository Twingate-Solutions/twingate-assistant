## Responsible Disclosure Policy

Twingate's policy for reporting security vulnerabilities. Outlines scope, rules of engagement, and reporting process.

**Reporting:**
- Email: security@twingate.com
- Describe the vulnerability in detail with reproduction steps
- Twingate acknowledges receipt within 5 business days; aims to resolve critical issues within 5 business days of disclosure
- Provide reasonable time before public/third-party disclosure

**Rules:**
- Only test accounts you own or have explicit permission to test
- Do not exploit vulnerabilities, disrupt services, violate user privacy, or alter/destroy data
- Do not target physical security measures
- For severe vulnerabilities (system access): report immediately and stop further testing
- Destroy all artifacts (POC code, videos, screenshots) after the bug report is closed
- Keep all disclosure communications confidential unless Twingate consents

**Out-of-Scope Endpoints:**
- twingate.com company site, docs.twingate.com, forum.twingate.com, status.twingate.com, help.twingate.com, trust.twingate.com
- Subdomains: email, packages, sales
- Third-party hosted sites (unless they expose an in-scope endpoint)

**Out-of-Scope Vulnerability Types:**
- DDoS, spamming, physical attacks
