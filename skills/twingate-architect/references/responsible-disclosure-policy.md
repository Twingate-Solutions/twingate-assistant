# Twingate Responsible Disclosure Policy

## Summary
Twingate's responsible disclosure policy outlines how security researchers should report vulnerabilities. Reports go to security@twingate.com, with Twingate targeting resolution of critical issues within 5 business days of disclosure.

## Key Information
- **Contact**: security [at] twingate.com
- **Critical issue resolution target**: 5 business days from disclosure
- **Acknowledgement SLA**: Receipt confirmed within 5 business days
- **All assessments are final**
- Vulnerability acknowledgements page available (linked from policy)

## Rules for Researchers
- Only test against accounts you own or have explicit permission to access
- Do not exploit discovered vulnerabilities for any benefit
- Keep all bug disclosure communications confidential unless Twingate consents to third-party sharing
- Destroy all artifacts (PoC code, videos, screenshots) after bug report is closed
- Report severe vulnerabilities immediately and do not proceed further with exploitation
- Bug prioritization and resolution approach is at Twingate's sole discretion

## Out of Scope Systems
- twingate.com (company site)
- docs.twingate.com
- forum.twingate.com
- status.twingate.com
- help.twingate.com
- trust.twingate.com
- Subdomains: `email`, `packages`, `sales`
- Third-party hosted sites (unless they lead to an in-scope weakness)

## Out of Scope Vulnerability Types
- DDoS attacks
- Spamming
- Physical property or data center attacks

## Reporting Requirements
- Email security [at] twingate.com
- Describe vulnerability in full detail
- Include step-by-step reproduction steps
- Do not include out-of-scope items in reports

## Gotchas
- Policy does **not** authorize access to any data that isn't yours, including user data or Twingate internal data
- Threatening behavior toward Twingate personnel is prohibited
- No public or third-party disclosure until Twingate has had reasonable time to resolve
- Most informational/marketing subdomains are explicitly out of scope — focus is on the core product/service
