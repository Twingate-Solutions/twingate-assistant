## Cancel Your Subscription

How to cancel automatic subscription renewal. Cancellation stops auto-renewal; access continues until the end of the current subscription term.

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Sign into the Admin Console
2. Settings → Billing → Manage Plan
3. Click on your subscription
4. Click Edit Subscription
5. Select the Twingate Starter plan
6. Click Update Subscription

**Key Information:**
- Cancellation = downgrading to Starter plan, which stops billing; not an immediate account termination
- Access continues through the end of the current subscription term after cancellation
