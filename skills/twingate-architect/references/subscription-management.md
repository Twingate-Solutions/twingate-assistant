## Subscription Management

Billing and subscription management for Twingate accounts. Licenses are based on total Users plus Service Accounts (pending and disabled users count; each Service Account counts as one license regardless of key count).

**Billing Cadences:**
- **Monthly** -- billed at renewal based on user count on the last day of the previous cycle
- **Annual** -- billed at renewal based on last-day count; mid-cycle additions incur prorated charges on the 1st of each calendar month; reductions apply at next renewal
- **Invoice** -- contact account manager for changes

**Common Operations (Settings → Manage Plan):**
- Upgrade/downgrade plan: Edit Subscription → select plan → Update Subscription
- Downgrade takes effect at the next billing date (not immediately)
- Check scheduled changes: Manage Plan → subscription → View scheduled changes (also allows canceling a pending downgrade)
- Change billing email: Settings → Manage Plan → Billing & Shipping Addresses
- Download past invoices: Settings → Manage Plan → Billing History (PDF format)
- Upgrade to Twingate Home: redeem promo code in billing tab

**Gotchas:**
- Billing transactions run at midnight UTC — charges may appear at end-of-month depending on timezone
- Enterprise plan changes require contacting sales@twingate.com
